# Product Vision

Nest aims to become a **personal life management system powered by AI**.

The goal is to simplify life organization by providing a single control center.

Instead of using many disconnected apps, users interact with a unified system that coordinates tasks, plans, habits, and goals.

---

# Target Users

1. Power users who want a structured life management system.
2. Users who want AI assistance to organize their lives.
3. Entrepreneurs and creators managing many projects.
4. People seeking better balance between life areas.

---

# Main Promise

Nest helps users:

- reduce mental chaos
- see their life clearly
- plan actions efficiently
- automate life management with AI

---

# Long Term Vision

Nest becomes a **Life Operating System** that:

- manages tasks
- coordinates calendars
- tracks habits
- stores life reflections
- manages goals
- interacts with AI assistants
