# Product Vision

Nest aims to become a personal life management system powered by AI.

The goal is to simplify life organization by providing a single control center.

Instead of using many disconnected apps, users interact with a unified system
that coordinates tasks, plans, habits, goals, calendars, and reflections.

## Target Users

1. Private users who want a structured life management system.
2. Users who want AI assistance to organize their lives.
3. Creators and entrepreneurs managing many parallel goals.
4. Families and friend groups who want shared planning.

## Main Promise

Nest helps users:

- reduce mental chaos
- see life clearly
- plan actions efficiently
- automate life management over time

## Collaboration Expansion

After solo-first launch, Nest expands to invite-based collaboration:

- shared plans and lists
- co-management of responsibilities
- permission boundaries for private vs shared areas

## Long-Term Vision

Nest becomes a personal life workspace that:

- manages tasks
- coordinates calendars
- tracks habits
- stores life reflections
- manages goals
- supports shared planning with trusted people
- integrates AI assistance by default in mature phases
