# Visual Direction Brief

## Product Character
- Brand feeling: calm utility with editorial precision
- Trust posture: grounded, capable, never chaotic
- Energy level: focused and warm, not playful noise
- Keywords: life operating system, clarity, momentum, reflection

## Visual Thesis
- Nest should feel like a premium personal command center for real life, not a
  generic productivity SaaS dashboard.

## Surface Notes
- Mobile: one primary job per screen, thumb-friendly action zones
- Tablet: split-view planning where extra width reduces taps
- Desktop: multi-panel flows for scanning, comparison, and planning continuity

## Explicit Avoids
- loud crypto-style neon
- card soup with equal visual weight
- childish habit-tracker aesthetics
