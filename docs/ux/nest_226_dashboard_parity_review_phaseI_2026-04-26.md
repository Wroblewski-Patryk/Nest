## Result

The dashboard is now calmer and compositionally closer to the canonical
reference than phase H. The remaining differences are still visible, but they
are mostly in visual character rather than in structural layout.

## What improved in Phase I

- The shell feels more restrained, especially in the left rail.
- The editorial entry is lighter and less product-heavy.
- The hero and `Now focus` pairing feels tighter and more balanced.
- The lower zone reads as a quieter continuation of the main story.

## Remaining Gaps

## 1. The founder reference is still more serif-led and ornamental

- The current dashboard is elegant, but still slightly more app-like than the
  painterly/editorial founder image.
- The title, task cards, and support rail still carry a touch more utility
  than the reference.

## 2. The dashboard is now in micro-finish territory

- Remaining work should focus on art direction nuance:
  - sidebar calm,
  - title typography finish,
  - ornamental detail balance,
  - support-rail softness.

## Recommendation

Continue with one more narrow `canonical micro-finish` pass for the dashboard
shell and then shift the same language to `Journal`, `Planning`, and `Life
Areas` so the product feels consistently canonical instead of having only one
hero screen at that quality level.
